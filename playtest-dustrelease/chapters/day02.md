TITLE: Day 2 — Two Hit Points
DATES: Academy, Year One · Day 2 · the morning after the leaf
---
> A cannon is only frightening until you learn how little powder it holds. Today Iwao learns it from the inside.

## 05:30 — The warm-up, already a habit
The morning molding comes easy — it always will. Three natures up the Ram seal, held steady, his mother's wheel turning behind him. He does not even open his eyes for it now. Breakfast; Mai's sparkly hands; the climb up the cliff-stair with Akemi narrating the whole village for him. Routine, on the second day, already wearing a groove.

## Morning periods — the shape repeats
Chakra Control: effortless. Conditioning: he finishes in the middle of the pack, lungs burning, while Ganryū laps the stair and pretends not to keep score. The pattern of the boy is setting like poured concrete — **godlike in the hands, painfully mortal everywhere else.** Nobody has yet seen what happens when those two truths collide in the same thirty seconds.

They are about to.

## 16:00 — The first sanctioned spar (Ryūen presiding) — ninjutsu permitted
Ryūen finally lets the gloves come off: a supervised match, techniques allowed, first decisive blow takes it. Iwao draws **Rōmaru** — loud, broad, desperate for a crack at the heir, and (the records are careful to note) no pushover: he comes to the circle with a real technique in his hands. *(Engine note: Rōmaru built as a kitted combatant — Earth Release: Earth Clone in his loadout, surfaced and ready. No improvised filler.)*

The bell. They roll even — and the tie falls to **Rōmaru.**

He slams his palms to the dirt and a slab of the yard rises into a mud-grey twin of himself — **Earth Release: Earth Clone** — and the clone is *already moving,* already inside Iwao's guard, and it hits like the cliff itself. *(Rōmaru casts his kitted jutsu — 6 earth damage. Iwao: 8 → 2 HP.)*

**Two hit points.** One more touch and the heir is on his back in front of the whole class. There is no time to be gentle the patient way, no time to wear Rōmaru down across a careful exchange. Iwao's body simply cannot afford a second hit. And so the two truths collide.

He breathes — *don't crush them, don't crush them* — and aims not to break the boy but to *end the moment*: **Wind Release: Sickle Weasel.** Cutting wind howls off his fingertips. The earth-clone bursts to gravel; Rōmaru himself is thrown flat and does not get up. *(Iwao hits AC 13; thirty-one points of slashing on the dice. Rōmaru down — a spar knockout, a half-second before Ryūen's shout.)*

The yard is silent. Iwao stands swaying at 2 HP over a classmate he very nearly hurt for real, and understands, all at once and forever, the exact thing his father told him at this same hour yesterday. *A heavier stone. Whether the man crushes the people beside him.* He almost did. On the second day.

## After — wary respect, and an empty tank
Rōmaru wakes rubbing his ribs and, to his credit, grins crooked: *"Okay. Okay, heir — you're not soft."* *(Rōmaru's regard shifts to wary respect.)* Iwao patches himself up under Ryūen's hard stare — *(short rest: +6 HP back to full; his single hit die spent; chakra still half-empty)* — but one spar has emptied him: half his chakra gone, every reserve burned. This is the lesson under the lesson. **His gift can win any single instant and survive almost none of them.** He goes home early, as he promised his mother.

## 19:30 — Dinner, and the lesson comes due
He doesn't brag. He tells them the *2 HP.* He tells them about the wind he had to throw to live, and how close Rōmaru's ribs came to something that doesn't mend. Tsuru holds him without a word. Gennai only nods — yesterday's sentence made flesh. *(Tsuru — defining; their bond crosses into real closeness.)* And somewhere in his mother's arms the boy makes the first true decision of his shinobi life: not to be stronger. To be **controlled enough that the strength protects the people beside him instead of crushing them.** That is a harder jutsu than any in the scrolls, and he will spend the whole story learning it.

## 22:30 — Sleep, and the eye that does not
*(Long rest: chakra 6 → 14, hit die restored, Will of Fire renewed.)* Above, the tick turns again: Ōnoki at the rail, Ryūen at his reports, Sōun spending quiet words. *(iwagakure → 5, iwa_academy → 5.)* The village leans a little further toward the boy who almost crushed a classmate to keep from falling. None of them were afraid of that today. Only Iwao was. That difference is the whole of him.
