TITLE: Day 3 — The Nature He Was Told Not to Touch
DATES: Academy, Year One · Day 3
---
> Everything comes free to Iwao except the one thing the whole village wants. Today he finds the edge of it, and it nearly takes a piece of him.

## Morning — the groove, and a new weakness found
The warm-up is reflex now; Mai's sparkly hands, the climb, Akemi's running commentary. Then a visiting instructor drops a layered **genjutsu** over the whole class — *find the seam.* Iwao cannot *cast* an illusion to save his life, but his analyst's mind tears this one apart from the inside, naming each layer where it fails to meet. *(WIS save 20 vs DC 13 — clean.)* The ledger of the boy gains another line: **weak where it's about feeling, formidable where it's about understanding.** A genjutsu-user could never trap him twice. A genjutsu-user could fool him *once,* though — and once is sometimes all it takes.

## 12:30 — Lunch, behind the equipment shed
Day 2's terror has not made him want *less.* It has made him want to *understand the nature no one will let him near* — because if Dust is going to nearly kill the people beside him, he has to learn to hold it. So he hides behind the shed, folds the Ram, and reaches past wind, past fire, past earth, for the place where all three are meant to fuse.

It does not come free. *(Chakra Control 11 vs DC 16 — failure.)* The three natures lurch and **catch wrong.** His fingertips go dead-numb; a coin-sized patch of the shed wall behind his shoulder sighs into grey powder and is simply *gone* — unmade, not broken. For one heartbeat Iwao tastes what the legend actually *is*, and it is not glory. It is **deletion**, and it does not care whose hand it's in.

This is the reframe the whole arc turns on: control was never the test. **Dust is the test.** The one nature that matters is the one thing that has ever refused his gifted hands.

## The shadow that falls — Ryūen
He never hears the sensei coming. **Ryūen** sees the numb fingers, sees the hole in the wall, and goes white to the lips. The forbidding is absolute — *never alone, never unsupervised, not for years* — but under the anger is naked fear: this is a man who has seen what half-formed Dust does to a child's body, and has carried that image a long time. *(Ryūen — defining; his protective regard deepens sharply even as he's furious.)* He marches Iwao back to the range and supervises an afternoon of deliberately *small* work — wind off the fingertips, earth into clean shapes, nothing fused — and does not look away once.

## Evening — the swallowed stone
Iwao says nothing to his mother about the wall. He lies awake turning it over: *everything is easy except the only thing they want, and the only thing they want could erase the people I love by accident.* He decides, again and harder, that he will not touch it again until he can hold it perfectly — and that perfect is very far away. *(Long rest — pools steady; Will of Fire renewed.)*

Above, the village leans closer regardless. *(Night tick, medium: Ōnoki watches — iwagakure → 7; Ryūen advances him — iwa_academy → 6.)* They are proud of a boy for a power he is, tonight, quietly terrified of.
