TITLE: Day 4 — If He Can't Touch It, He'll Read It
DATES: Academy, Year One · Day 4
---
> Forbidden the bloodline in his hands, Iwao goes at it the only way that has ever obeyed him: with his head.

## Morning — the discipline of the small
After the scare he is a model student of the *small* natures: wind held thin and clean, earth shaped without force, fire kept to an ember. Tetsuna watches him stop *showing off* and start *refining,* and marks it — the gift learning humility. The classes blur in their honest groove; legs still mortal, hands still uncanny, mind already somewhere else.

## Free period — the Academy archive
Where his mind has gone is the stacks. He talks a wary archivist into the oldest restricted Dust theory a first-year is allowed near, and reads like a starving thing. *(History 19 vs DC 15 — success.)*

And he finds the key. Dust fusion is not **sequence** — it is **simultaneity.** Three natures held in perfect, equal balance *at the same instant,* a lattice the old texts call the *particle frame.* That is why Day 3 failed: he threaded wind-then-fire-then-earth, the way he cut the leaf. To hold Dust he must hold all three *together,* forever, without a single flicker — and the texts are blunt that this takes most people *years,* and breaks the ones who rush it. For the first time the legend has a *shape* he can study, even if his hands can't yet hold it. **His mind is the one road to the bloodline that the village's impatience cannot pave for him.**

## 18:00 — "Brains need dumplings"
He would have read until the lamps died if **Akemi** hadn't hauled him out by the collar, declared that brains need dumplings, and made him be eleven years old for one hour at the food stalls. She feels the new weight riding his chakra and — to her great credit — does not pry. She just keeps him *human,* which is the precise and quiet jutsu she is best at. *(Akemi — bond deepens; the counterweight to the archive.)*

## Evening, and the ledger that fills while he sleeps
Home, more notes by lamplight, the particle frame sketched and re-sketched in a margin. Sleep. *(Long rest — pools steady; Will of Fire renewed.)* Above, the nightly tick: Ōnoki at the rail, Ryūen at his reports. *(iwagakure → 9, iwa_academy → 7.)* The village's certainty about the heir grows in exact proportion to the heir's own dawning understanding of how far he has to go.
